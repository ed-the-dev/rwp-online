Graduate Careers

RWP currently has vacancies for graduate engineers to join our team in Slough. We accept graduates with at least a 2:1 MEng in Civil Engineering.

Upon beginning your career, you will be involved in one of many varied projects. We offer a wide variety of design, site based and management experience, allowing graduates to develop their engineering and leadership skills, ready to become Chartered Engineers.

Our Graduate Training Scheme is fully accredited by the Institute of Civil Engineers (ICE) and you will be encouraged and supported towards achieving Professional Chartered Engineer status.

Please email us your CV with ‘Graduate Careers at Robert Walpole’ in the subject line for further information.
